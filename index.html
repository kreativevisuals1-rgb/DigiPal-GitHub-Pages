FAQ

Common Questions

How does DigiPal work?⌄

DigiPal is an all-in-one platform. Create a free account, fund your wallet, and access VTU services, digital courses, leads generation, the marketplace, and more from a single dashboard.

Is it free to sign up?⌄

Yes! Registration is completely free. You only pay when you use services or purchase products. No hidden monthly fees for basic access.

Which networks are supported for VTU?⌄

We support all major Nigerian networks: MTN, Airtel, Glo, and 9mobile — for airtime and data bundles. Cable TV includes DStv, GOtv, and StarTimes.

How quickly are transactions processed?⌄

All VTU transactions are processed instantly — typically within seconds. Our platform guarantees 99.9% uptime.

Can I earn money on DigiPal?⌄

Yes! Earn through our referral program, selling on the marketplace, reselling VTU services, and completing digital skill courses that open higher-income opportunities.

Is my money and data safe?⌄

Absolutely. We use bank-grade SSL encryption to protect all transactions and personal data. Your wallet funds are secured and we never share your information with third parties.
